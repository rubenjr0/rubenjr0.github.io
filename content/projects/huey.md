Change the colors of any image to match a desired palette, leveraging the [OKLAB](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oklab_color_space) colorspace to maintain perceptual uniformity.

Available at [crates.io](https://crates.io/crates/huey) and [github](https://github.com/rubenjr0/huey).
